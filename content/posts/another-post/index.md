Consistency matters more than perfect posts.

This is the small workflow I use to keep publishing:

1. Capture a quick idea in one sentence
2. Expand into three sections: problem, approach, result
3. Add one concrete example or snippet
4. Publish the same day if possible

For technical blogs, clarity beats cleverness. A short post that solves one real problem is better than a long draft that never ships.

I will keep refining this workflow as I publish more here.
