I use this structure for fast weekly updates.

## What I learned

- Concept 1 and why it matters
- Concept 2 and where I applied it

## What I shipped

- Feature/fix completed
- Result or impact

## What blocked me

- Main blocker
- How I resolved it (or next step)

## Next week focus

- Priority 1
- Priority 2

Short logs help track progress and create useful content without writing long essays.
